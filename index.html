🏰 Stuffy War 🏰

Two-player castle battle · same keyboard · first to 0 HP loses

How it works
  • Each castle starts at 1,000 HP. You earn gold every second (base +5/s).
  • Spend gold on five attacker types: ⚔️ Sword (cheap frontliner) · 🗡️ Ninja (fast double-sword, glass cannon) · 🏹 Archer (snipes from across the whole screen) · 🛡️ Tank (huge HP, soaks damage for units behind it) · 🔥 Fire (splash damage specialty).
  • Buy 🍌 Banana Farms to boost income. Farms I and II are one-time upgrades; Farm III is repeatable — stack as many as you want for runaway gold.
  • Cards up top glow green when you can afford them. If you press a key without enough gold you'll see a "Not enough money!" warning.
  • Toggle sound with the 🔊 button (bottom-right) or press M.
